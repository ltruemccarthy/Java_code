About Me

I'm a designer based in Minneapolis, MN. My love for user centered design comes from the idea that the closer we get to design that feels natural, intuitive and unobtrusive, the easier it becomes to inspire, engage and enable people far and wide. This area of design allows me to do all of my favorite things: push boundaries, experiment with technology, collaborate, experiment with style and art and highlight the brilliant work of others. The tech and design world continues to amaze me with its capabilities and generous culture. It pushes me to learn and play daily.
